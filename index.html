About Me

I'm Muhammad Idrees, a Lecturer of Mathematics at Government Boys Postgraduate College Quetta, and alongside that I'm building a career as a software developer. Teaching has sharpened the way I think — breaking complex problems into clear, logical steps and explaining them simply — and I bring that same mindset to writing code.

My journey into tech grew out of a love for problem-solving and a curiosity about how software actually works under the hood. I've been learning web development and, more recently, agentic AI, enjoying the process of turning ideas into fast, functional web apps. I work across the stack, from crafting clean UIs to building the logic that powers them.

Balancing a teaching career with hands-on development keeps me grounded in both the theoretical and practical sides of problem-solving. Whether I'm explaining a mathematical proof to a classroom or debugging a tricky piece of code, I approach it with the same patience, curiosity, and attention to detail — and I'm excited about where combining education, mathematics, and AI-driven development can take me next.
